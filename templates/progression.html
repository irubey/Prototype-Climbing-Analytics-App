Difficulty Categories - Pitches/Boulders climbed or attempted categorized by relative difficulty.
Project Climbs - Pitches/Boulders climbed at or above climber's peak performance at the time.
Tier 2 Climbs - Pitches/Boulders climbed 1 grade below climber's peak.
Tier 3 Climbs - Pitches/Boulders climbed 2 grade below climber's peak.
Tier 4 Climbs - Pitches/Boulders climbed 3 grade below climber's peak.
Base Volume - Any pitches/boulders graded below tier 4, considered base volume.
